Meet Di

I've been planning Disney for the legacy traveler since 2019.

My name is Diane Mack and I own Travel Creations By Di, LLC. I've been a dedicated travel advisor with a deep passion for all things Disney, but my heart truly belongs to the legacy travelers. I know what it's like to crave the magic without wanting to run a twelve-mile-a-day marathon. I believe some of the most profound Disney moments don't happen on a roller coaster — they happen over a perfect meal, while strolling through world-class gardens, or in the quiet glow of a grandchild's face during the fireworks.

My Mission for You

Travel built for your pace.

I'm humbled every time a family or a solo traveler trusts me with their vacation. I'm not here to "book a room." I'm here to act as your strategist and advocate, ensuring your trip is paced for memories, not checklists.

Beyond the Parks

Disney is my first love. The world is my second.

While Disney is my first love, my expertise extends to the rivers of Europe and the world's most beautiful ports. Whether we're planning a castle-side character breakfast or a luxury river cruise through the heart of Germany, my goal remains the same — seamless, sophisticated, stress-free travel.

I work especially well with Disney Cruise Line and Adventures by Disney clients. The same care that makes a Magic Kingdom day work for an after-60 traveler — pacing, accessibility, the un-Googleable details — is exactly what makes a 14-day European river journey feel effortless instead of exhausting.

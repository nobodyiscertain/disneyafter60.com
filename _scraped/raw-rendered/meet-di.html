Meet Di: Your Partner in Purposeful Magic

"Why Disney After 60?" I get asked this all the time. The answer is simple: Because that’s me!

My name is Diane Mack and I own Travel Creations by Di, LLC. I've been a dedicated travel advisor with a deep passion for all things Disney since 2019, but my heart truly belongs to the "legacy" travelers. I know what it’s like to crave the magic without wanting to run a 12-mile-a-day marathon. I believe that some of the most profound Disney moments don’t happen on a roller coaster—they happen over a perfect meal, while strolling through world-class gardens, or in the quiet glow of a grandchild’s face during the fireworks.

Travel Built for Your Pace

I’m humbled every time a family or a solo traveler trusts me with their vacation. I’m not just here to "book a room." I’m here to act as your strategist and advocate, ensuring your trip is paced for memories, not just checklists.

My Mission for You:

  • Technology Tamed: I navigate the confusing apps and booking systems so you don't have to stress over your phone.

  • ​Comfort-First Planning: I specialize in "low-walking" routes, the best resting spots, and accessible logistics.
​
  • Curated Experiences: From the "Grand-Gathering" multi-generational family trip to a peaceful solo adventure through EPCOT’s festivals, I tailor the magic to your physical needs and personal interests.

Beyond the Parks

While Disney is my first love, my expertise extends to the rivers of Europe and the world's most beautiful ports. Whether we are planning a castle-side character breakfast or a luxury river cruise through the heart of Germany, my goal remains the same: Seamless, sophisticated, and stress-free travel.

Insights for the Informed Traveler

I believe that a well-prepared traveler is a happy traveler. That’s why I maintain a dedicated Travel Blog—my personal vault of tips, tricks, and "information to ponder" as we plan.







Whether you’re curious about the specific accessibility of a Disney Cruise Line ship, the immersive luxury of an Adventures by Disney guided tour, or just need to know which EPCOT pavilion has the most shade, I’ve written or will be writing about it. I dig into the details of:

Disney Cruise Line: Navigating the ports and finding the best "Adults Only" sanctuaries on board.

Adventures by Disney: Why these world-class guided tours are the ultimate way for legacy travelers to see the world.

Pro-Tips: Everything from mastering the latest technology to the best time of day for a quiet stroll through the Magic Kingdom.

Disney After 60!

What the heck is “Disney After 60” and who am I to talk about this?!

A little introduction - My name is Diane Mack. I own Travel Creations by Di, LLC and have been creating magical vacations professionally for over 7 years now. I have been creating Disney vacations for my family and friends for well over 30 years. My passion is creating that memory making trip of a lifetime that exceeds the expectations of those I create these magical experiences for.

The first time I visited Disneyland was in 1970 when I was 14 years old and that was it! Hooked!! I spent the summer of 1974 in the Santa Ynez Valley in California on an apprenticeship training Arabian horses and went to Disneyland every month while I was out there. Needless to say, I started taking my children to Walt Disney World when they were 7 and 9 years old. Now I’m a Nana and don't get me wrong, we LOVE going to Walt Disney World with the kids and grandkids, but we also look forward to our trips “our way”! I’m now 69 and my husband is 79 (but do not call us Senior Citizen’s, LOL!) and we love every minute of every day at Disney World! Nowadays, it has a different look and feel, but it’s still as thrilling as the very first time I went as a kid.

Let’s start with WHAT Disney After 60 is. It is a safe haven for all of us adults that love Disney, have loved Disney for years and years and despite our age, STILL love going to Disney, be it now on our own terms! We no longer have to worry about keeping up with the kids or grandkids, racing from attraction to attraction. We can stop and see a show or walk through an exhibit without being tugged on and hearing the whining voices saying, “Hurry up, I want to ride the next ride!” We acknowledge that we have EARNED the right to our trips being a bit slower paced but still action-packed with everything we might have missed or been hurried through when traveling with family, while not leaving out all the thrills we’ve always loved.

Why do I feel compelled to discuss this niche of Disney travelers? Because I AM one! My husband and I have been a few times now by ourselves and we love how we do Disney on our terms. These trips are very different than trips with our family and I recently realized (as I am in the middle of planning a week at Animal Kingdom Lodge for us in the spring of 2026) that I am not seeing this particular subset of adults that love Disney being addressed. So, here I am in all my glory to announce, Disney After 60 is awesome!

Do I want to be called a “Senior”, nope! Do I acknowledge that my trips look and feel different than they did 30 or 40 years ago - absolutely! And the best part of that epiphany is that, IT’S OK!!! As I’m writing this, I’m hearing in my head the melody of Leslie Gore’s 1963 hit, “It’s my Party” and giggling that “It’s my Vacay and I’ll do it my way!”

Join us and stay tuned as we share, discuss and discover all the nuances of Disney After 60!! You won’t want to miss out!
